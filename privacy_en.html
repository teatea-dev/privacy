TeaTea Privacy Policy

TeaTea (“the App”) delivers gentle, supportive messages to brighten your day. To provide this experience, the App handles only the minimum amount of information necessary. The App does not collect any personally identifiable information.

1. Information We Collect

The App handles the following information:

  • Reminder times (morning, afternoon, evening)
  • Theme information required for message generation (time of day, seasonal context, etc.)

2. Information Stored on Your Device

Reminder times and generated messages are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences. This information is managed entirely on your device and is not shared automatically.

3. Information Sent Externally

To generate messages, the App may send minimal theme-related data to an external server. This data does not include your name, email address, device identifiers, or any information that can personally identify you.

4. Sharing with Third Parties

The App does not provide user information to third parties unless required by law.

5. How to Delete Your Data

All data stored on your device will be removed when you uninstall the App.
